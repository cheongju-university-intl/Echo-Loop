1. App Purpose

Echo Loop is an English listening and speaking practice app for intermediate and advanced learners. Users can import English audio, add subtitles manually by uploading an SRT file or by using AI transcription, and complete a 4 stage study flow: Blind Listening, Intensive Listening, Shadowing, and Paragraph Retelling. The app also schedules spaced repetition reviews automatically.

2. Instructions for Reviewing

No login or account is required. All data is stored locally on device.

To review the app:

1. Open the app and tap the "+" button in the Library tab to import any .mp3 or .m4a audio file.
2. Open the audio menu and tap "Manage Subtitles" to upload an SRT subtitle file, or use AI Transcription.
3. Go to the "Study" tab and open the imported task.
4. Complete the 4 study stages: Blind Listening, Intensive Listening, Shadowing, and Paragraph Retelling.
5. After completion, spaced review tasks will appear automatically on the Study tab.
6. The "Favorites" tab contains bookmarked sentences and saved words.
7. Tap any word in subtitles to view definitions, phonetics, and contextual analysis.

3. External Services

Backend API at https://www.echo-loop.top is used for AI transcription, translation, grammar analysis, and word analysis.

Cloudflare R2 is used only for temporary audio upload during AI transcription.

Vercel AI SDK is used in the backend to integrate with Alibaba's Qwen LLM for AI powered features (transcription, translation, grammar analysis, and word analysis).

Apple Speech Framework and Apple NaturalLanguage Framework are used on device for speech recognition and text similarity during pronunciation practice. The microphone and speech recognition permission prompts will appear when the user enters the Shadowing stage. On device text to speech is used for word pronunciation playback. No audio data is sent to external servers for these features.

No third party authentication, payment, or analytics services are used.

4. Regional Differences

The app works the same in all regions. The UI supports English and Simplified Chinese based on device language.

5. Regulated Industry

This app does not provide regulated services.
